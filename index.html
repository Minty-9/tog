FAQ

Common Questions

What types of foodstuff do you export?

We export a wide range of Nigerian foodstuff, including dried fish, garri, fufu, spices, palm oil, crayfish, and more.

How long does shipping take?

Our international deliveries are completed within 3–5 business days, depending on your location.

What catering equipment do you rent?

We offer plates, bowls, cutlery sets, serving spoons, glasses, chafing dishes, and more for your events.

Which areas do you serve for catering rentals?

We serve Lagos, Ogun, and Oyo states for all catering equipment rental bookings.

How do I place an order?

You can place an order through our website or by contacting our customer service team via email or social media.

Do you do custom packaging?

Yes! We offer custom packaging options tailored to your specific needs. Contact us to discuss your requirements.
